About Us

Wolf Wizard is a platform for artists where one can network, volunteer, shop/book services or products and indulge in events. We aim to bridge the gap between the artists and the service providers and also, an effective way of networking along with finding collaborations easily in a detailed way.

We, at Wolf Wizard, have always been keen on indie artists and have been focusing on how to help the artists out there in their music journey and to make it easy as well as worthwhile. On that note, we bring to you a platform where you can avail many features like Music Services, showcasing your indie profile and also networking.
