About Us

Wolf Wizard is a platform for artists where one can network, volunteer, shop/book services or products and indulge in events. We aim to bridge the gap between the artists and the service providers and also, an effective way of networking along with finding collaborations easily in a detailed way.

We, at Wolf Wizard, have always been keen on indie artists and have been focusing on how to help the artists out there in their music journey and to make it easy as well as worthwhile. On that note, we bring to you a platform where you can avail many features like Music Services, showcasing your indie profile and also networking.

Truth be told, there are lots of problems faced in the music industry and with this pandemic around, it has become very difficult. We feel you, fam and would like to make it better for you.

Our Founder

When I started my journey as a Lyricist and A&R three years ago, I've found myself in various hardships and as someone who has just begun her music journey in a new city, networking was an issue and there came several problems along with it. That's when I discovered an idea to create something that would solve the problems that indie artists face. I believe that age is just a number and we all are capable of doing stuff that we love as long as we have confidence and a vision in life.

Vaishnavi M R
Founder of WolfWizard
